# a pushed status expires after its TTL and Tab cycles the ring

### Given

- a fresh app
- a status "a transient notice" was pushed onto the ticker

### When

- time passes beyond the status TTL on the virtual clock
- I clear events
- I press Tab to advance the ticker ring
- I press Tab again to advance to the 'hints' provider

### Then

- the status shows the input hint
- a TickerCycled event was emitted
